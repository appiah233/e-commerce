Our heritage

The art of Ghanaian beadwork

From the heart of Accra to your doorstep

EmyAfroBeads was born from a passion for preserving the ancient art of Ghanaian beadwork. Every bead in our collection tells a story — from the recycled glass bottles carefully crushed and melted in traditional kilns, to the intricate patterns that echo centuries of African heritage.

Our artisans in Accra pour their hearts into each piece, blending time-honored techniques with contemporary designs. We believe luxury should be sustainable, meaningful, and accessible to everyone who appreciates authentic craftsmanship.
